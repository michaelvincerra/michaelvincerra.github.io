DataPanino™

Don't just crunch your data. Munch your data.™

DataPanino™ helps you discover trends in economic growth by comparing Intellectucal Property (IP) sales with overall economic growth in seven (7) leading European Union countries. Chart visualization shows a country's growth— from 1975 to 2015—and allows you to save custom snapshots, using four economic indicators:

    ►  IP: Intellectual Property Sales
    ►  GNI:  Gross National Income
    ►  GDP: Gross Domestic Product
    ►  FDI: Foreign Direct Investment
  Identify trends and compare growth by sector. Select a range of years. Save a snapshot in your collection.
(The Netherlands [NL] with 4 indicators are shown here.)

Global economic data shows explosive growth of 5435.6% from 1975-2016 for sales on patents, copyrights, and trademarks— collectively—intellectual property (IP). Compared with other economic indicators, IP sales presents a useful predictor for understanding how countries innovate, develop, and design new ideas—as well as license and sell branding and publishing. This web application serves as a tool for analysts, economists, and investors.
